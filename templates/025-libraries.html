Plex does not officially support blank collections, which is what separators are, and you may experience bugs if you enable Separators.

Remove Overlays will remove all Overlays from your library - you do not need to unselect previously selected Overlays as this toggle will take priority.

Reset Overlays will grab a new base image from the source that you choose, and then use that as the source for applying your Overlays

Read this Warning:


Tip: Using Reset Overlays or frequently Removing and Reapplying overlays will very quickly result in Image Bloat, as neither Plex or Kometa will clean up previously-used posters with Overlays. We would advise you to look at using ImageMaid to clean up old, unused images
